How Goals work

Goals — what you want to do

  • Add them with + Add Goal.
  • Status: Idea (someday) → Active (committed) → Achieved (done), or Dropped.
  • Optionally tag Years (e.g. 2026, 2027) to plan when — the goal then appears under each of those years.

Achieving a goal

  • Set status to Achieved and pick a date.
  • It writes a Journal entry for that day and shows up in the Years view 🎯.

Finding things

  • Filter the list by Year or Status.
  • Goals with no year sit in the Backlog section.
